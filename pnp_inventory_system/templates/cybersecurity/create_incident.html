Incident Reporting Guide
What to Include
  • Date & Time: When the incident occurred
  • Affected Systems: Which assets/users were impacted
  • Impact Description: What happened and how it affects operations
  • Immediate Actions: What steps were taken initially
  • Evidence: Any logs, screenshots, or documentation
Quick Reporting Tips
Report Immediately: Don't delay reporting security incidents.
Incident Types
  • Malware: Virus, ransomware, spyware infections
  • Unauthorized Access: Intrusion attempts, suspicious logins
  • Data Breach: Unauthorized data access or exfiltration
  • Phishing: Email or website spoofing attempts
  • Hardware Theft: Physical theft of equipment
  • Other: Any other security-related incident
Next Steps

After reporting an incident:

  1. Incident will be logged and tracked
  2. Appropriate personnel will be notified
  3. Investigation will begin based on severity
  4. Resolution actions will be documented
  5. Follow-up monitoring will be implemented
